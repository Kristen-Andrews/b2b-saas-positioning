B2B SaaS Positioning Canvas

A one-page framework for owned category positioning. Fork it, fill it out, ship better copy.

Why this exists

Most B2B SaaS companies can't answer three questions in a single sentence:


What category are you in?
Who is this specifically for?
What do they do today if not you?


If those three answers aren't tight, nothing downstream works. The website is generic, the sales pitch drifts, the ads underperform, and the board asks why marketing isn't producing pipeline.

This canvas forces the answers.

What's in here


CANVAS.md is the template. Fork this file, fill it out, commit it to your team wiki.
examples/ has two worked examples for fictional companies, one dev tools, one fintech.
stress-test.md is a validation checklist. Run your completed canvas against it before locking.


How to use it

Solo pass: 90 minutes. Fill it out in one sitting. Don't polish. First draft is diagnostic, not final.

Team pass: 3-hour working session with product, sales, and marketing leadership. Debate section by section. The disagreements are the point.

Board pass: Present the completed canvas as your positioning strategy. Every strategic marketing decision downstream should trace back to it.
